hOw tO buY

  1. ⁠⁠

    Download Phantom Wallet

    • Visit the Phantom website and download the Phantom wallet extension for your browser.
    • Create a new wallet and save your recovery phrase in a secure place.

  2. Get Solana (SOL)

    • Visit the Phantom website and download the Phantom wallet extension for your browser.
    • Transfer the purchased SOL to your Phantom wallet.

  3. Go to Raydium

    • Visit the Raydium website and connect your Phantom wallet.
    • Paste the REAGAN token address into Raydium.
    • Select REAGAN and confirm.
    • When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign the transaction.
    • Paste the REAGAN token address into Raydium again, select REAGAN, and confirm.
    • Sign the wallet signature prompt from Phantom.

  4. ⁠Swap SOL for REAGAN

    • On Raydium, select SOL in the “From” field and REAGAN in the “To” field.
    • Enter the amount of SOL you want to swap for REAGAN
    • Confirm the swap and approve the transaction in Phantom.
